Medical Management

Advanced care for
every patient’s need

At our clinic, we focus on medical management as the first line of care. This means treating your health concerns with the right medicines, simple lifestyle changes, and regular monitoring, rather than moving straight to surgery and preventive guidance to reduce the chances of the problem coming back. Our aim is to help you recover comfortably and manage your health in the safest, most effective way possible.

In medical management, different specialists such as doctors, nurses, physiotherapists, and pharmacists work together to provide the right care. They not only focus on reducing symptoms but also guide patients about healthy habits and future precautions. This combined approach ensures that patients feel better and continue living a healthier life.

Spinal Injection

Caring for every
patient with trust

A spinal injection is a safe and effective procedure we use to relieve pain and inflammation directly around your spine. It is especially helpful if you are experiencing back pain, neck pain, or nerve-related discomfort. By delivering the medicine close to the affected area, this treatment often provides quick relief without the need for surgery, making it a trusted option for many of our patients.

During the procedure, the doctor uses X-ray guidance (fluoroscopy) to ensure the injection is placed at the exact spot. The patient may receive mild anesthesia to stay comfortable, and the entire process is usually quick and safe. With the help of spinal injections.

Keyhole Spine Surgery

Your health is our
first priority

Keyhole spine surgery, also called minimally invasive spine surgery, is a modern method where the surgeon operates on the spine through very small cuts in the skin. Unlike traditional surgery, which requires larger incisions, this technique reduces damage to the surrounding muscles and tissues. As a result, patients usually experience less pain and quicker recovery after the operation.

This type of surgery is done with the help of special instruments and advanced imaging systems that allow the doctor to clearly see the affected part of the spine. Since the cut is very small, the chances of infection and blood loss are also lower.

Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery

We always take care
our patient

Minimally invasive spine surgery (MISS) is a modern technique used to treat problems of the backbone with very small cuts instead of large incisions. Because the opening is smaller, there is less damage to nearby muscles and tissues, which means patients feel less pain and recover more quickly after surgery.

In the older method of spine surgery, doctors had to make a big cut and move or even remove some tissue to reach the spine. MISS avoids much of this by using special instruments and advanced imaging systems to reach the problem area with minimal disturbance.

Conventional Open Spine Surgery

Quality treatment with
personal attention

Conventional open spine surgery is the traditional method used to treat serious spine problems and injuries. In this procedure, the surgeon makes a larger cut on the back or abdomen to directly reach the spine. This gives the doctor a clear view of the affected area and allows for treatment of complicated conditions that may not be possible through minimally invasive techniques.

Although recovery may take longer compared to smaller surgeries, open spine surgery is still a very effective and safe option for many patients. It is often chosen for complex cases where detailed work is needed on the spine.
