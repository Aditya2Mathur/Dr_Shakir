Spine Surgery

“Advanced treatments to restore mobility, relieve pain, and improve spinal health with precision care.”

Spine surgery focuses on treating conditions such as slipped discs, spinal stenosis, fractures, and deformities that affect movement and quality of life. With modern techniques and minimally invasive procedures, we aim to reduce pain, restore function, and ensure faster recovery for patients.

  • Minimally invasive spine procedures for quicker recovery
  • Treatment for spinal trauma and fractures

Neurology

"Comprehensive care for brain, spine, and nerve disorders with precision and trust."

Neurology focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. From headaches and epilepsy to strokes, movement disorders, and neuropathies, expert neurological care helps patients manage symptoms and improve their overall quality of life.

  • Diagnosis and management of stroke.
  • Treatment for epilepsy and seizure disorders.
  • Care for chronic headaches and migraines.

Headache & Migraine

"Specialised care to diagnose, treat, and manage chronic headaches and migraines effectively."

Headaches and migraines can greatly impact daily life, affecting work, concentration, and overall well-being. While occasional headaches may be common, recurring or severe headaches often indicate underlying neurological conditions that require expert attention.

  • Diagnosis of chronic headaches and migraines.
  • Personalised treatment plans for lasting relief.
  • Identification and management of triggers.

Nerve Disorders

"Comprehensive care for nerve compression, injuries, and functional neurological problems."

Nerve disorders can cause pain, weakness, numbness, or loss of function, often affecting daily life and mobility. Conditions such as nerve compression, neuropathies, or injuries require expert diagnosis and treatment to prevent long-term complications.

  • Diagnosis and treatment of peripheral nerve disorders.
  • Care for nerve compression such as sciatica or carpal tunnel.
  • Management of neuropathies and nerve injuries.

Stroke Management

"Timely diagnosis and treatment to reduce risks and improve recovery after stroke."

Stroke is a medical emergency that occurs when blood flow to the brain is interrupted, leading to damage of brain cells. Early recognition and immediate treatment are critical to reduce complications and improve the chances of recovery.

  • Emergency care for acute stroke management.
  • Advanced imaging and diagnosis for quick decision-making.
  • Medication and surgical options to restore blood flow.
